The briefing gap

Your rankings are fine.
Your traffic is falling.

For twenty years, brand visibility meant search ranking. That era is ending. AI has become the first research stop for B2B purchasing decisions — and most brands have no idea what it says about them. SEO explains your Google position. It cannot explain what AI recommends to your prospects.

The counterintuitive truth

"Your AI brand was written by
people you've never met."

AI learned your brand from the aggregate of everything ever written about you — third-party articles, analyst reports, review sites, forum threads. Your website, your campaigns, your carefully crafted messaging: a footnote. The version AI hands to your prospects every single day was assembled entirely without your input — and until now, no one could read it.

Three dimensions

What AI currently
knows about your brand

Understanding

Does AI accurately describe what your brand does, who it serves, and what it stands for? Misunderstanding at this level means every AI recommendation rests on a broken foundation — and you have no idea it's happening.

Memory

How confidently and consistently does AI recall your brand across different queries and AI systems? Weak memory means your brand disappears the moment a competitor is stronger in AI's training data — silently costing you recommendations.

Association

What concepts, values, and competitors does AI connect to your brand? Association shapes every recommendation. Being linked to the wrong ideas — or missing the right ones — directly costs you deals before a conversation begins.

Annual research

The General
Visibility Score

Each year, the Institute for Brand Salience Research (IBSR) publishes the General Visibility Score (GVS) — a benchmark ranking 500 brands across Understanding, Memory, and Association in every major AI system. It is the first scientific standard for measuring what AI knows about brands — and the basis for every CMO's AI brand strategy.

  • 500 brands measured across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity
  • Industry benchmarks and competitive intelligence by sector
  • Year-over-year salience tracking and trajectory scoring
  • Certified methodology reviewed by independent academic partners

How it works

From invisible
to intelligible

01 · Scan

Read the brief

We query every major AI system with hundreds of brand-relevant prompts. We capture exactly what AI currently believes about your brand — in its own words, unprompted, across all contexts.

02 · Measure

Score and compare

We score your brand across Understanding, Memory, and Association — benchmarked against your direct competitors and your industry average in the ABMI Index. You see where you stand.

03 · Report

Own the brief

You receive your complete AI Brand Brief — what AI says, where it's wrong, what competitors do better, and a prioritised action plan for improving your score before the next Index publishes.
